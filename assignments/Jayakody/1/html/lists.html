Resolution
The ability to distinguish two closely spaced objects as separate entities, typically measured in nanometers for advanced microscopy techniques.
Numerical Aperture
A dimensionless number that characterizes the range of angles over which a microscope objective can accept light, directly affecting the resolution and brightness of the image.
